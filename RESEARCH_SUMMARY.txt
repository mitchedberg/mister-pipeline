  └─ 0 custom graphics ICs
  └─ 68000 + Z80 (standard)
  └─ Straightforward tile/sprite blitting
  └─ Estimated: 50-80 days

Toaplan V2 (Simple + Proven)
  └─ Extends V1 (reference available)
  └─ 68000 + Z80 (standard)
  └─ Likely incremental graphics from V1
  └─ Estimated: 60-90 days

Psikyo (Complex but Well-Documented)
  └─ 4 custom graphics ICs (PS2001B, PS3103, PS3204, PS3305)
  └─ 68EC020 + Z80 (well-understood)
  └─ All custom chip behaviors reverse-engineered in MAME
  └─ Estimated: 80-120 days

Kaneko 16 (Very Complex)
  └─ 7 custom ICs (VU-series, VIEW2, etc.)
  └─ MCU co-processors (CALC3, TOYBOX) — risky
  └─ High-color background processing
  └─ Estimated: 70-100 days (HIGH UNCERTAINTY due to MCU)

DECO32 (Highest Complexity + Risk)
  └─ ARM CPU (encrypted, requires key extraction)
  └─ H6280 sound (not yet in jotego cores)
  └─ Custom chips poorly documented (DE 141/74/99 render pipeline unclear)
  └─ Estimated: 120-180 days (HIGH RISK)
